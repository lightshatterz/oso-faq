How do I get roles?

Type "?f" (without quotes) in any channel. You will be presented with a list of roles. Then, you have to type ?f with the role you want.

Why I can't post in #animators-corner?

You haven't got the Animator role yet. You have to type "?f animator" without the quotes in any channel.
